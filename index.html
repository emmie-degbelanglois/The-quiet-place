Our concept

Let inspiration come to you

Our concept tea shop is made to help you come up with your greateast pieces. Wheter you're a painter, a writer, a poet or a photograph, all artists are welcome. We designed quiet and themed rooms, thoughfully arranged so their aesthetic inspire you to navigate through your work. Discover themed playlists in function of the desired mood of your piece, wheter it is contemplation, horror, or many more. All of this while enjoying our latest teas, coffes, or home-made deserts.
